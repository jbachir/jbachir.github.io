About Me

Jimmy is an data analyst who is motivated by the belief that data can be used to make better decisions in all aspects of life. He wants to make data more engaging and easy to understand so it can be used efficiently. Jimmy is a strong advocate of open data and open source software. Jimmy primarily uses SQL, Python, but has experience with R as well.

Jimmy has worked in developing a new lead system to help his company thrive after medical equipment was needed during the pandemic.
